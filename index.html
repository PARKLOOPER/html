This tool is designed to support mail carriers in locating and identifying Cluster Box Units (CBUs) and other mailbox-related delivery points. Users are required to ensure that any photos taken and uploaded are from publicly accessible areas and avoid trespassing on private property or photographing from restricted areas. This tool is solely intended for capturing images of mailboxes, CBUs, and delivery points; users should avoid capturing images of people, private residences, any identifiable personal information, or access codes. All images uploaded are publicly viewable, and by using this tool, carriers agree to have taken them lawfully and with respect for privacy, without any intent to intrude upon anyone's personal space. Any images found in violation of these terms will be subject to removal. Please keep our platform positive and respect the privacy of others.
